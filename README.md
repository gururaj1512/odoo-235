# 🏆 QuickCourt – Your Local Sports Booking Platform

QuickCourt is a smart platform designed to bridge the gap between sports enthusiasts and local sports facilities.  
From booking a badminton court to tracking live tournament scores, QuickCourt ensures **easy access**, **real-time updates**, and **seamless management** for **users, facility owners, and administrators**.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement
Booking sports facilities is often a **time-consuming**, **manual**, and **fragmented** process.  
Players struggle with:
- Finding nearby courts at the right time  
- Weather-related scheduling issues  
- Limited visibility of real-time availability  
- Lack of digital convenience for owners & admins  

---

## 💡 Our Solution
QuickCourt is an **end-to-end sports facility booking platform** that:
- Helps players discover and book courts in real-time
- Provides weather-based suggestions for optimal playtime
- Offers a voice-assisted and chatbot-driven experience
- Empowers owners with analytics & booking management
- Enables admins to ensure quality & moderation across the platform

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js, TailwindCSS, TypeScript
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **Voice & Chatbot:** Web Speech API, Speech Recognition Kit
- **Maps & Weather:** Google Maps API, OpenWeather API  
- **Analytics & Charts:** Chart.js / D3.js
